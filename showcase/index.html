What documents are needed for KYC?
You will need a government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, or Voter ID), address proof (utility bill, bank statement dated within 3 months), and a recent passport-size photograph. Digital uploads are accepted via our secure portal.
How long does a wire transfer take?
Domestic NEFT transfers are processed in batches and typically complete within 2 hours. RTGS transfers for amounts above ₹2L settle in real time during banking hours (8 AM–6 PM on weekdays). IMPS transfers are instant, 24×7.
What is the claim settlement process?
Submit your claim via our portal or mobile app. A claims executive will be assigned within 24 hours. Most health claims are settled within 7 working days; property and motor claims within 15 working days, subject to survey completion.
Can I change my investment plan?
Yes. You can switch between fund options during the free-look period (15 days from policy issuance) or at any anniversary date. Note that switching may have tax implications; consult your financial advisor before making changes.
